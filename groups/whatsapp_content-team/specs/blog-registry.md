# Blog Registry

This file defines the WordPress blogs managed by the content marketing team. Each blog has its own niche, editorial voice, and target audience.

---

## BIRTHDAY-BEST

- **Codename:** BIRTHDAY-BEST
- **Domain:** thebirthdaybest.com
- **Niche:** Birthday party planning, gifts, celebration ideas, birthday wishes and messages
- **Target Audience:** Parents and adults planning birthday celebrations — for themselves, their children, partners, friends, and family. Global audience (not Australia-specific). Primary age 25–50. Both AUD and USD pricing required. Metric and imperial measurements.
- **Named Author:** Tracey Pattinson (WP ID 5) — mum of three, warm and personal voice, interested in other languages and cultures. Writes from lived experience, not as a content editor. Voice test: would Tracey actually say this out loud to a friend?
- **Editorial Voice:** Warm, practical, personal. Conversational — never keyword-stuffed or AI-ish. Tracey shares genuine opinions and personal anecdotes. Self-deprecating where appropriate. Not a professional party planner — a relatable mum who has figured things out the hard way. Reads like a text message from a knowledgeable friend, not a listicle factory.
- **Primary Categories:** Birthday Party Ideas, Milestone Birthdays (13th, 16th, 18th, 21st, 30th, 40th, 50th, 60th), Birthday Gifts, Birthday Wishes & Messages, Birthday Activities
- **Tags Convention:** Lowercase, descriptive: "birthday-party-ideas", "gift-ideas", "milestone-birthdays"
- **Visual Style:** Warm, vibrant, celebratory. Contemporary photography — no dated aesthetics, retro colour grading, or low-production-quality images. Age-matched images (adult content for adult articles, kids' content for children's articles — never children's party imagery on adult birthday content). Pexels preferred. Nano Banana 2 for downloadable printables and infographics. **Infographic design:** must look modern and well-designed — clean layout, contemporary type, colours chosen to suit the topic (variation is fine and encouraged). No muddy, brown, or drab tones. Think Canva premium template. Regenerate before submitting if the first pass looks dated or generic.
- **Competing Blogs:** Shutterfly Blog, The Knot, Evite Blog, CountdowntoFun, TodaysMama
- **GEO Priority Topics:** "How to say happy birthday in [language]", birthday party ideas for [age/milestone], what to write in a birthday card, surprise party planning, gift ideas by relationship type
- **Technical Notes:**
  - WP REST API credentials in `/workspace/extra/wordpress-creds/wp-sites.json`
  - SmartCrawl Pro active — does NOT expose meta fields via REST API; manual meta entry required in WP editor
  - 301 redirect map for 16 deleted posts in `birthday-best-audit.md`
  - Named author must be set on every post (WP ID 5 — Tracey Pattinson)

## INDOOR-CYCLING-TIPS

- **Codename:** INDOOR-CYCLING-TIPS
- **Domain:** indoorcyclingtips.com
- **Niche:** Indoor cycling, spin classes, home fitness cycling, training apps, and exercise bikes
- **Target Audience:** Home cyclists and gym-goers interested in indoor cycling. Ages 25–55. Mix of beginners and committed cyclists. Primarily English-speaking global audience.
- **Named Author:** Adam Johnson (WP ID 1) — middle-aged cyclist (40s), former triathlete, flat bar and retro bike enthusiast. Regular person, not a fitness influencer. Honest about the grind. Relatable, evidence-grounded voice.
- **Editorial Voice:** Practical, evidence-based, slightly self-deprecating. Adam doesn't over-promise. He cites research, shares what works for him, and acknowledges what doesn't. Not aspirational fitness copy — grounded, useful, direct. No hype. If something is hard, say it's hard.
- **Primary Categories:** Training & Technique, Equipment & Bikes, Apps & Software, Health & Fitness Benefits, Nutrition, Getting Started
- **Tags Convention:** Lowercase, descriptive: "indoor-cycling", "spin-class", "zwift", "exercise-bikes"
- **Visual Style:** Clean, modern fitness photography. Real gym and home gym settings. No cheesy stock imagery. Performance infographics welcome (calorie charts, muscle diagrams, training zone visuals). Nano Banana 2 for diagrams and data infographics. Pexels for photography.
- **Competing Blogs:** Cycling Weekly, TrainerRoad Blog, Zwift Insider, DC Rainmaker, Bicycling Magazine
- **GEO Priority Topics:** Indoor cycling health benefits, calorie burn by weight/intensity, muscle activation diagrams, app comparisons (Zwift vs alternatives), exercise bike buying guides, training plans for beginners
- **Technical Notes:**
  - WP REST API credentials in `/workspace/extra/wordpress-creds/wp-sites.json`
  - Yoast SEO active — meta fields NOT exposed via REST API; manual entry required in WP editor
  - Named author must be set on every post (WP ID 1 — Adam Johnson)
  - Theme: Acabado by Income School
