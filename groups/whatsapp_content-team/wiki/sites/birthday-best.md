# Birthday Best (BIRTHDAY-BEST)

**Domain:** thebirthdaybest.com
**Platform:** WordPress
**Author:** [Tracey Pattinson](../authors/tracey-pattinson.md)
**Status:** Active — consolidation and rewrite phase underway (Apr 2026)

---

## Niche and Positioning

Birthday Best covers birthday party planning, gifts, celebration ideas, and wishes/messages across every relationship type and milestone age. Global audience — US, UK, Australia, and beyond.

The site's positioning is a research-authority model: Tracey Pattinson writes as a thorough researcher and relatable mum. She doesn't claim to have personally tried every idea, but she researches everything carefully before recommending it. Authority comes from rigour and relatability, not professional credentials.

**What the site is not:** A professional party planning service, a product catalogue, or an AI listicle factory.

---

## Target Audience

- Parents and adults planning birthday celebrations (for themselves, children, partners, friends, family)
- Primary age: 25-50
- Global — both AUD and USD pricing required, metric and imperial measurements
- Readers want practical ideas that actually work in real life, not just on Instagram

## Key Editorial Rules

- Both AUD and USD pricing on all product/gift recommendations
- Age-matched images — never use children's party imagery in adult articles
- First in-article image in the upper third of the article
- Infographics summarising article content go immediately after the TL;DR
- Images must match the specific section subject, not be generic celebration photos
- Longer listicles (10+ items) need 2 in-article photos
- Internal link anchor text must sound like Tracey talking — never an editorial signpost
- No em dashes in any content

---

## What Has Worked

High-scoring articles (95+/100) share these characteristics — see [What Works](../learnings/what-works.md) for full analysis:

- Structured with a clear definition-style opening, punchy TL;DR, and FAQ section
- Specific, concrete examples rather than generic advice
- Organised by relationship type or milestone (not just a flat list)
- Voice consistently warm and personal throughout — reads like Tracey talking
- Pillar-page structure covering multiple subtopics under one hub URL

**Specific high scorers:**
- Birthday Letters for Boyfriend (98/100) — 15 letter templates with distinct voice per situation
- Birthday Party Supplies Checklist (96/100) — highly specific, practical, well-structured
- Birthday Wishes for a Friend (96/100) — 11 friend categories, genuine warmth
